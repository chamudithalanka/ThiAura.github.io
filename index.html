ThiAura International Pvt Ltd

ThiAura International Pvt Ltd promotes wellness for the body, mind, and soul. In an increasingly demanding world that impacts mental, physical, and emotional well-being, our company was created to offer health and wellness services that encourage a holistic lifestyle for our clients. ASTANGA YOGA

Ashtanga Yoga is a dynamic practice that synchronizes breath with a progressive series of postures. This method produces intense internal heat, purifying the body through sweat, improving circulation, flexibility, and strength.

Hatha YOGA

Hatha Yoga is a branch of yoga focusing on physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), and meditation to balance the body and mind, enhancing overall well-being and inner peace.

Read More

IYENGAR YOGA

Iyengar Yoga, developed by B.K.S. Iyengar, emphasizes precision and alignment in poses. It often uses props like belts, blocks, and blankets to aid in achieving perfect form and to accommodate all levels of ability.

Read More

RESTORATIVE YOGA

Restorative yoga is a style of yoga that encourages physical, mental, and emotional relaxation. Appropriate for all levels, restorative yoga is practiced at a slow pace, focusing on long holds, stillness, and deep breathing.

QIHONG YOGA

TAI CHI YOGA

Tai chi is an ancient Chinese martial art. Initially developed for combat and self-defense, it has evolved into a sport and form of exercise. Tai chi is a gentle, low-impact form of exercise in which practitioners perform a series of deliberate, flowing motions while focusing on deep, slow breaths.

PILATES YOGA
